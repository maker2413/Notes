Amazon is an American multinational tech company that focuses on e-commerce,
cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and
[[id:1685a9c2-92ca-4d13-9438-c343769474de][artificial intelligence]]. Amazon was initially an online marketplace for books,
however it has expanded into a multitude of product categories. Some of these
products include:
- [[id:f53e00a8-3d5b-44f6-ae31-c5b507f2d87b][AWS]] (Amazon Web Services)
- Zoox (Autonomous vehicles)
- Kuiper Systems (Satelite internet)
- Amazon Lab126 (Computer Hardware R&D)
- Amazon Prime Video
- Amazon Music
- Audible
- Amazon Publishing
- Amazon Studios
- Kindle e-readers
- Echo devices
- Fire tablets
- Fire TVs

Amazon has also acquired many companies such as:
- Ring
- Twitch
- IMDb
- Whole Foods Market
- [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer][Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]]
