#+last_modified: [2023-04-04 Tue 20:56]
#+filetags: Concept

EOL, or End of Life, is a term used to describe a product which has reached the
end of its life and will no longer be recieving updates. This indicates that the
product may no longer be useful and is potentially unsafe to use as it is no
longer getting security updates.
