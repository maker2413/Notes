#+title: Grub

#+last_modified: [2022-02-06 Sun 12:41:37]
#+filetags: Linux Tool

~grub~ is the most commonly used boot manager and simply handles how your
operating systems boot. It is capable of support multiple operating systems on
one machine, commonly referred to as "multi-boot", and allows the user to select
between the installed operating systems at boot.
