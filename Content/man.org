The ~man~ command is used to display the manual of the command or tool you
specify. For example to get the manual of the [[id:a18196a7-adf8-4d86-af49-bb4915c366ca][ls]] command you would just need to
run: ~man ls~. From there the manual will be displayed and you can scroll
through it and press ~q~ to close the manual.
