Microsoft is an American multinational tech company headquartered in Redmond
Washington. Microsoft's best know product is the [[id:9020d898-7f38-4d9d-a928-562566e8a2e6][Windows]] operating system,
however they have other products like:
- Microsoft Office
- Internet Explorer/Edge
- Microsoft Surface

They also are the owners and creators of the Xbox game consoles. Some of the
companies Microsoft has bought up through the years include:
- [[id:0c164614-cdac-4936-aca3-a6cb340883fa][GitHub]]
- LinkedIn
- Skype
