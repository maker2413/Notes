operating system that [[id:8f8d4797-dca7-4e7f-afcb-b12fa196d412][Linux]] was trying to reenvision back in the day. It can be
said that ~unix~ designed much of [[id:8f8d4797-dca7-4e7f-afcb-b12fa196d412][Linux]]'s design philosophy because of this.
